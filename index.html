Triveni Resort

Triveni Riverside Resort is a unique and luxurious resort located in Sundar Haraincha-4 Dulari. Surrounded by serene rivers and lush greenery, Triveni Riverside Resort offers a tranquil and peaceful escape from the hustle and bustle of city life.

One of the highlights of the resort is its boating facility, which allows guests to explore the picturesque river and take in the stunning views. The resort also features one-of-a-kind igloo rooms, offering guests a truly unique and memorable experience.
